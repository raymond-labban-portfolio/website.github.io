Content Management System (CMS)

A CMS (Content Management System) is a software application that allows users to create, manage, and publish digital content on the internet without requiring extensive technical knowledge or coding skills. CMS's provide a user-friendly interface that enables individuals and organizations to easily organize, edit, and update their website's content.

The primary purpose of a CMS is to streamline the content creation and publishing process by separating the content from the presentation layer. This means that users can focus on creating and editing the content, while the CMS takes care of presenting that content in a consistent and structured manner on the website.


WordPress

WordPress is a popular content management system (CMS) and website creation tool. It is an open-source software that allows users to build and manage websites, blogs, and online stores without requiring advanced coding skills.

E-Commerce

E-commerce, short for electronic commerce, refers to the buying and selling of goods and services over the internet. It involves online transactions conducted between businesses (B2B), between businesses and consumers (B2C), or between consumers (C2C), facilitated through various digital platforms and technologies. E-commerce has revolutionized the way businesses operate and consumers shop, allowing for greater convenience, accessibility, and global reach.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It refers to the practice of optimizing a website or online content to improve its visibility and ranking in search engine results pages (SERPs). The ultimate goal of SEO is to attract more organic (non-paid) traffic to a website by making it more relevant and appealing to search engines and their users.

Website Hosting

Web hosting refers to the service of providing the infrastructure and resources necessary for a website to be accessible on the internet. When you create a website, the files, images, videos, and other content that make up your site need to be stored on a server connected to the internet. Web hosting companies offer servers and related services to ensure your website can be accessed by users around the world.
